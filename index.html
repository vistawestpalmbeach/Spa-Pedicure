What Makes a Spa Pedicure Different?

A standard pedicure focuses mainly on cleaning, trimming, and polishing nails. A Spa Pedicure, however, takes the experience to the next level by including additional treatments such as:

  • Exfoliating scrubs to remove dead skin
  • Hydrating masks to soften feet
  • Cuticle care for healthier nails
  • Calming massage to improve circulation and reduce stress
  • Long-lasting polish or gel application for a flawless finish

These extra steps not only enhance appearance but also provide therapeutic benefits that leave you feeling refreshed and renewed.
